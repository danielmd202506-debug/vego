# Product

## Register

product

## Users

VEGO shoppers who are comparing raised garden bed kits on the website. They are often planning a patio, side yard, or backyard layout and need confidence about size, shape, color, add-ons, and the final buying list before they add to cart or ask support.

## Product Purpose

The workspace supports VEGO website experience concepts, design-system assets, and conversion-focused prototypes. The 3D garden bed configurator demo helps shoppers assemble one modular raised bed kit visually, understand the footprint and recommended add-ons, then continue to cart or send the setup to support.

## Brand Personality

Helpful, garden-smart, calm, and premium. The experience should feel like a practical garden planning assistant rather than a technical CAD tool or a decorative marketing toy.

## Anti-references

Avoid dark gaming-style configurators, over-technical CAD interfaces, generic ecommerce card grids, hidden CTAs, decorative motion, and 3D effects that slow down the purchase task.

## Design Principles

1. Make the buying decision easier: every control should answer a shopper question about fit, layout, color, or add-ons.
2. Keep the 3D view useful before it is impressive: the model should clarify the kit and its footprint quickly.
3. Preserve VEGO's garden identity: use natural greens, calm surfaces, and product-led composition.
4. Turn configuration into a sales handoff: the selected setup should become a cart payload, quote, email, or support summary.
5. Design mobile first: garden shoppers often browse from phones while planning a real outdoor space.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, clear focus states, reduced-motion support, and button labels that describe the action. Color choices should not be the only indicator of selected or disabled states.
